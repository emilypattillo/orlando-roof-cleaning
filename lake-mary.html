Frequently asked questions

Can you work inside gated communities like Heathrow?

Yes — gate access is arranged at scheduling, and HOA documentation (dated after-photos, invoice) is a standard part of closing out a notice in communities with active enforcement.

I have a tile roof. Does that change anything?

It changes the method: tile work is planned around minimal walking and edge application, because tiles crack underfoot. Mention tile in the quote request and the plan adjusts from the start.

How often do Lake Mary roofs need cleaning?

Open, sunny lots stretch the cycle; the older canopy sections shorten it. Communities with active HOA inspection effectively set the schedule — a few years between cleanings is typical.
